FAVORITE CARTOONS

Tom and Jerry" is a classic animated cartoon series featuring a mischievous cat named Tom and a clever mouse named Jerry,
created by William Hanna and Joseph Barbera, where the main plot revolves around Tom's relentless attempts to catch Jerry,
leading to hilarious and often slapstick chases with minimal dialogue, making for a visually comedic experience;
Tom and Jerry has won several Academy Awards and remains a staple of animation history, inspiring generations of animators and still being loved worldwide.
the series is renowned for its iconic characters, exaggerated situations, and physical humor, with many episodes showcasing Jerry's quick wit outsmarting Tom's plans.

Motu and Patlu are two friends living in Furfuri Nagar.
Motu Patlu is a big hit in India and is widely regarded for its simple but engaging storylines that appeal to young viewers. The characters' quirky personalities and funny situations make it easy for kids to relate to, and it's also enjoyable for families.
Samosas are Motu's favorite food, and he frequently tries to steal them from a small shopkeeper named
Chaiwala who makes the best samosas in the town, along with selling tea.
After Motu eats samosas, he temporarily gains more energy and strength.

Cartoons like Tom and Jerry and Motu Patlu have a special place in the world of animated entertainment.
These shows are beloved by kids (and even adults) around the world for their humor, fun characters, and memorable storylines.
